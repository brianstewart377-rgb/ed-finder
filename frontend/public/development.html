Development Model Notes

ED Finder now ranks systems through archetype-led development assessment. Finder, Compare, Map selection, Pinned, Watchlist, and System Detail all treat development score as the primary operator-facing signal.

Current rule: the app should describe systems in terms of development score, archetype fit, buildability, purity, and slot headroom.

What The Development Score Means

The development score is the top-line view of how promising a system is for colony development. It is anchored to the strongest colony archetype for that system rather than to a legacy economy-pair score.

  • What colony role does this system fit best?
  • How strong is that fit?
  • How buildable is the system once slot and body constraints are considered?
  • How clean or contaminated is the archetype profile?

How Development Tuning Works

Development Tuning re-ranks the current Finder results with archetype-weighted factors:

  • Purity
  • Buildability
  • Slots
  • Expansion
  • Logistics

Mechanics Reference

The colony mechanics background still comes from the same Elite Dangerous colonisation research and repository documentation. The app contract now expresses those mechanics through archetypes and development potential.
