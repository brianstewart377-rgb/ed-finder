Scouter's Priority Checklist

▼
  1. Tier S — Black Holes, Neutron Stars, or White Dwarfs
  2. Tier S — Water Worlds or Earth-Like Worlds (especially as primary port)
  3. Tier A — Ammonia Worlds with large orbital slot count and NO RINGS
  4. Tier A — Pure Rocky Bodies (no signals, no volcanism) with 4+ surface slots
  5. Tier B — Pure Icy Bodies (no signals) OR Icy + Geo only, 3+ surface/1+ orbital
  6. Tier B — Terraformable Rocky with Bio signals but NO Geo or volcanism
  7. Tier C — Pure HMC/MR (no signals) with high surface slot count
💡 Four solid systems within 15 LY of each other beats one perfect system 50 LY away. Always prioritize proximity over perfection!
